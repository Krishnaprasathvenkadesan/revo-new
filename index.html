About REC & ECE

The Electronics and Communication Engineering Department started in 1997. It offers courses for UG, PG, and Ph.D. students. From the beginning, the department has focused on research, helping students become skilled researchers. Each year, over 95% of eligible students get placed in top companies. The department has been producing skilled engineers who can compete globally since it began.

Rules & Guidelines

On spot registration is not entertained.

No refund on the registration fee will be entertained.

Participants are strictly advised to refrain themselves from using abusive language, hate speech and politics inside the campus.

We have zero tolerance policy on consumption of tobacco, liquor and drugs inside the campus once found guilty harsh steps will be taken on the participants.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for the events?

+

Registration can be done through our online portal. Click on the 'Login' button at the top of the page, create an account, and follow the registration process for your desired events.

Can I participate in multiple events?

+

Yes, you can participate in multiple events as long as there are no schedule conflicts. Make sure to check the event timings before registering.

What should I bring on the event day?

+

Please bring your college ID, registration confirmation, and any specific materials required for your registered events. Details will be provided in the registration confirmation email.

Is accommodation provided?

+

Yes, accommodation can be arranged upon request. Please mention your requirement during registration. Additional charges may apply.

Are there any team events?

+

Yes, several events allow team participation. Team size and composition requirements vary by event. Check individual event details for specific information.

Will certificates be provided?

+

Yes, participation certificates will be provided to all participants. Winners will receive additional certificates and prizes.
